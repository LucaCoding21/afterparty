Prefer minimal, clean styling. No borders, outline boxes, or divider lines to separate sections. Differentiate through typography (weight, color, size) and generous whitespace instead.

**Why:** User rejected bordered cards and line dividers — they feel heavy. Stussy (ca.stussy.com) is a reference for the aesthetic: black-on-white, uppercase labels, understated CTAs (underlined text > bordered buttons), lots of breathing room.

**How to apply:** When styling sections or differentiating content, reach for spacing, font weight, and color contrast first. Avoid decorative borders, background fills, and outlined buttons unless explicitly requested.
